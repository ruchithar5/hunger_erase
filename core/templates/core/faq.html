Hunger Erase is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger by connecting donors who have surplus food with NGOs and communities in need.

To donate food, register as a donor, login to your account, and fill in the donation form with details such as description, quantity, pickup address, and optionally upload a photo of the food.

Registered NGOs and community organizations in our platform receive notifications about available food donations and coordinate pickup and distribution to those in need.
